TYS POS 7.1 updates

1. Purchases: inventory dashboard cards removed.
2. POS: daily sales date filter now shows every transaction for the selected day plus transaction count, items sold and total revenue. Profit is deliberately not shown on POS.
3. Purchases: optional selling-price update with an explicit checkbox. Previous completed sales remain unchanged.
4. Expenses: name search, category filter, from/to dates, clear and filtered CSV export.
5. Owner dashboard: best/worst sellers, restock priorities, best days/times/month and inventory health.
6. Product varieties: custom name, selling price and stock deduction.
7. Corrections & Adjustments: stock and cost corrections with reasons and history.

New pages: dashboard.html, variations.html, adjustments.html
